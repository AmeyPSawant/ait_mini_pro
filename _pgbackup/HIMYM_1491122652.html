HOW I MET YOUR MOTHER

A comedy about Ted, a successful urban architect in his late 20s who is obsessed with finding his soulmate.

Ted lives with his closest friends, a young lawyer and a bubbly kindergarten teacher who are engaged to marry. Each acts as an advice-giver and a shoulder for Ted to cry on about his love life. Using a framing device where years later an off-screen Ted recounts each episode to his kids, the format is one long tale of HOW I MET YOUR MOTHER.
